## Symbol Elements

### Shield Shape
- **Style**: Modern shield with curved bottom
- **Inner Accent**: 60% opacity overlay

### Droplet Symbol
- **Shape**: Teardrop with rounded top
- **Size**: 40% of icon width

### E Letter
- **Style**: Bold, geometric
- **Alignment**: Centered in droplet

### Sparkles
- **Count**: 4 (top, bottom, left, right)
- **Size**: 2-3px radius circles
