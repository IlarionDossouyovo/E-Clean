# E-Clean Brand Logo

Premium brand identity for E-Clean, a high-end international e-commerce and dropshipping platform specializing in cleaning products, equipment, and hygiene solutions.

![E-Clean Logo](src/logo-preview.png)

## Brand Overview

- **Name**: E-Clean
- **Tagline**: Premium Cleaning Solutions for Modern Living
- **Part of**: ELECTRON Group ecosystem
- **Markets**: Africa, Europe, International B2B & B2C

## Design Concept

### Logo Symbol
- **Shield**: Protection & hygiene
- **Droplet**: Freshness & cleanliness  
- **Sparkle**: Excellence & quality
- **E Letter**: Brand identity integration

### Color Palette

| Color Name | Hex Code | Usage |
|------------|---------|-------|
| Primary Blue | `#0A2540` | Trust, technology |
| Secondary Cyan | `#00C2CB` | Freshness, cleanliness |
| Eco Green | `#2ECC71` | Sustainability |
| White | `#FFFFFF` | Clarity |

### Typography
- **Primary Font**: Inter (Bold 700)
- **Secondary Font**: Inter (Regular 400)
- **Weights**: 300 (Light), 400 (Regular), 700 (Bold)

## Usage Guidelines

### ✅ Do
- Use the correct color variation for background
- Maintain clear space (padding) around logo
- Use minimum sizes: 32px for icon, 120px for full logo

### ❌ Don't
- Stretch or distort the logo
- Add effects (shadows, glows)
- Change colors outside brand palette
- Rotate the logo
